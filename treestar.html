About the facilitator

Danielle Verrilli

Danielle is a Registered Teacher, a PACFA registered Clinical Counsellor and Wellbeing Educator who has had over 28 year's experience teaching and counselling, supporting children and their families to achieve psychological health and social and emotional wellbeing with individual and family counselling, implementing targeted intervention programs and consultation.

Some of the scope of her work has included supporting children and their families with concerns around worries, stress, low mood, sleep issues, identity, managing emotions, regulation, relationships, family separation, problematic online behaviour, focus and concentration, grief and loss, transition, social skills development, building resilience, developing self-esteem and confidence, self-compassion.

Danielle is a Co-Director of Cybersafe Families. Cybersafe Families provide online safety education for students and their families, school staff, local community, sporting and corporate groups. Through educational programs they support children and families with the challenges of this ever changing platform, and help them to navigate their online world with safety, respect, responsibility and empathy.

While currently working as a counsellor and wellbeing educator in schools, Danielle also runs a small private clinic at Evandale where she facilitates small groups and provides counselling for children and their families.
